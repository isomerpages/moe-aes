### AES Learning Festival

On 22 May, AES kick-started our very own Learning Festival, jointly organised by the Mathematics, Science and Humanities Departments! The secondary 1 students participated in either The Amazing Race or the PET Rocket Competition, conducted by secondary 2 and 3 YSP members respectively. During the Amazing Race, the students moved around the school in search of clues to find their “missing friend”! Using an open-source app, which is accessible from the ipads, the students had a lot of fun using their brain juices to solve Mathematics, Science and Humanities problems.

### Youth Science Ambassador Program

During the June Holiday, 5 of our YSP students participated in the Youth Science Ambassador Programme organised by the Singapore Science Centre. They used the Design Thinking Process to analyse the needs of the elderly and came up with an app Talking Wheelie to help them.
