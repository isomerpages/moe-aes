<h3><u>Direct School Admission (DSA)</u></h3>
<p>Dear Parents/Guardians and Primary 6 students,</p>
<p>Direct School Admission for secondary schools (DSA-Sec) allows students
to apply for a place in the school based on their talents and achievements.</p>
<p>Assumption English School (AES) will be offering Direct School Admission
in the following areas of talent:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Basketball (Girls &amp; Boys)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Community Youth Leadership (Girls &amp; Boys)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Debating &amp; Public Speaking (Girls &amp; Boys)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Floorball (Boys)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Football (Boys)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Judo (Girls &amp; Boys)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Softball (Girls)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>We invite students who wish to grow their talent and passion in these
areas to become a part of our AES family in 2027.</p>
<p>The selection criteria and process for the talent areas will be updated
from early-May. For more information on DSA-Sec, please refer to the MOE
website at <a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/dsa-sec" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.moe.gov.sg/dsa-sec</a>
</p>
<p>You may access the following link&nbsp;<a href="https://www.assumptionenglish.moe.edu.sg/useful-resources/direct-school-admission-dsa/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">AES DSA</a> for
specific details on how to apply to AES once DSA applications open in early-May.</p>
