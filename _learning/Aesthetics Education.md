<p>Aesthetics Education in AES provides opportunities for students to experience
various genres of the arts through the Art and Music education, aesthetic
school programmes, aesthetic CCAs and other performance platforms in school.</p>
<p>Through engaging students in aesthetic education, they are able to ignite
their creativity and develop their spirit to cultivate their artistic discipline
that strengthens their character and holistic development.</p>
