<h3><strong>Is there a teacher you know who is inspiring?</strong></h3>
<h4><strong>Please nominate your teachers for the following awards.</strong></h4>
<p><strong><u>PRESIDENT’S AWARD FOR TEACHERS (PAT)</u></strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Teachers who</strong>
</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; are mentors to you or/and your peers in
your/ their moral, character and citizenship development,</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; design learning experiences, including
the effective adaptation of educational technology, that motivate, challenge
and inspire you to realise your full potential</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; are role models for other teachers</p>

<p><strong><u>OUTSTANDING YOUTH IN EDUCATION AWARD (OYEA)</u></strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Teachers who</strong>
</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; are 35 years old and below in 2025</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; are mentors to you or/and your peers in
your/ their moral, character and citizenship development,</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; design learning experiences, including
the effective adaptation of educational technology, that motivate, challenge
and inspire you to realise your full potential</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; are role models for other teachers</p>
