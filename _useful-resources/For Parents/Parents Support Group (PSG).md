<p><strong><u>A</u>ssumption <u>C</u>o-<u>E</u>ducator<u>S</u> (ACES)</strong> was
formed for the purpose of helping each AssumptionKnight achieve their best.
With PSG, parents will have the opportunities to volunteer, socialise,
learn and share about parenting, have lots of fun, engage in family bonding
and make more friends.&nbsp;</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong><u>Objectives of AES Parent Support Group (PSG):</u></strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Provide a platform for parents to network and to get to know their children’s
classmates and teachers, as well as other parents;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Encourage parents to nurture positive family relationships with their
children through parenting workshops, school activities, etc.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Support strong engagement through the school structure so as to enrich
the learning experiences of AssumptionKnights.</p>
</li>
</ul>
