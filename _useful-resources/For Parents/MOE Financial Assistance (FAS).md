<p>Dear Parents / Guardians</p>
<p>Should you wish to apply for financial assistance and meet the MOE FAS
income criteria, please refer to the instructions below.</p>
<p><strong><u>Choose 1</u></strong> of 3 ways to apply for MOE FAS:</p>
<p>1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Submit via eFAS (<a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/moe-efas</a>)</p>
<p>2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Download the MOE FAS application form
(<a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.moe.gov.sg/financial-matters/-/media/files/financial-matters/moe-fas-application-form.pdf</a>).
Submit the completed form via the schoolâ€™s email address.</p>
<p>3.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Collect a copy from the school and submit
the completed form directly to the general office.</p>
<p>The application process usually takes up to 1-2 weeks. The school will
inform you on the outcome of the application.</p>
