**MTL Department Objectives:** <br>
To develop students who are confident and proficient users of their Mother Tongue Language, who appreciate the value of learning the language and knowing about our Asian culture and heritage.

**2) MTL Cultural Bites Series**

  

**Objective:** Through meaningful sharings, Assumptionknights will be able to gain a deeper understanding and respect for one another, regardless of their religion and ethnicity, to come together as one Assumption family.

The Cultural Bites Series are just-in-time, bite sized sharings of the beliefs and significance of the different ethnic and religious communities in the school either through morning assembly sharings, the aes_manna on Instagram or hands-on workshops. 

This year, due to the COVID-19 guidelines, while the hands on workshops were not able to be carried out, the MTL Cultural Bites Series went on social media to our aes\_manna instagram to share about relevant cultural information for our Assumptionknights.

Last year, a Kuih-Muih Making Workshop was organised for our secondary 1 to 3 Assumptionknight as part of the Hari Raya Aidilfitri celebration. They made delicious ondeh-ondeh and badak berendam and got to bring the traditional Malay desserts home to share with their family and friends!
