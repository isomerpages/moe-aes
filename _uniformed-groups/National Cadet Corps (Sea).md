**<u>AES National Cadet Corp (SEA)</u>**

  

AES NCC (SEA) is one of the 17 sea units in Singapore which is supported by the Republic of Singapore Navy (RSN). As a member of the NCC (SEA), our cadets have the opportunity to learn from the best and gain valuable insights from servicemen of the Republic of Singapore Navy.

  

One of the highlights as a cadet in NCC (SEA) is the opportunity to participate in the Midshipmen Sea Training Deployment (MSTD) where they sail with the RSN officers onboard RSS Resolution.

  

MSG Lim Kar Zun Daniel and SSG Wan Hon Jun took part in MSTD 2019, where they sailed with the RSN from Singapore to Jakarta for 6 days. Through this experience, they learnt to appreciate that the water security of Singapore is achieved through the hard work and vigilance of RSN. He also experienced first-hand about navy traditions ceremonial activities. Till date, AES NCC (SEA) has managed to send 3 cadets to participate in this once-in-a-lifetime event.

  

Beside MSTD, our cadets take the NCC (SEA) to a whole new level of adventure through active participation in water-based activities. Our cadets embrace the fun and challenges of kayaking, rowing, sailing and diving, and undergo basic training to attain certifications and badges, recognised and endorsed by the Singapore Canoe Federation and NCC Headquarters.

  

Driven by the 8 core values of the NCC, we continuously challenge our cadets to be the best that they can be, through drills, individual field craft, physical training, and sea activities such as kayaking, dragon boating and sailing.
